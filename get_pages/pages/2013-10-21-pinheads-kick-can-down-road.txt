			<h1 itemprop="headline">Pinheads: Kick the can down the road</h1>
			<div class="article-info">
			<div> 		
			 		<time itemprop="datePublished" pubdate datetime="2013-10-18T00:00-04:00">Published October 18, 2013</time><div itemprop="sourceOrganization" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization">FOX News</div></div>

			 <div class="m video-ct" data-video-id="2753578532001">
							<a href="http://video.foxnews.com/2753578532001/pinheads-kick-can-down-road" itemprop="url"><img	src="http://a57.foxnews.com/global.fncstatic.com/static/managed/img/fn2/video/876/493/101813_oreilly_pinheads2_640.jpg?ve=1&tl=1" itemprop="image"></a>

						<p><em>This is a RUSH transcript from &quot;The O'Reilly Factor,&quot; October 18, 2013. This copy may not be in its final form and may be updated.</em></p>
<p><em>Watch &quot;The O'Reilly Factor&quot; weeknights at 8 p.m. and 11 p.m. ET!</em></p>
<p>&quot;Back of the Book&quot; segment tonight, the &quot;Pinheads of the Week.&quot; And we begin with Gutfeld's selection of actor Chris Noth.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED HUFFINGTON POST REPORTER: If President Obama is white, do we have a government shutdown.</p>
<div></div>
<p>CHRIS NOTH, ACTOR: Probably not. I don't think they would have -- I don't believe it.</p>
<p>I mean, with these Tea Party guys, maybe, maybe it was inevitable but there is something about Obama that makes these people just intolerable. And I consider then to be un-American.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK, now, we had Robert Redford on yesterday. I don't know whether you saw the FACTOR, but he said he was racist, too. I don't know what these guys are drinking. I mean, what is that.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: I have a theory. You know, that was the &quot;Huffington Post,&quot; by the way. It's the blog for cat ladies and beta males.</p>
<p>Keep wondering, why do actors keep doing this. And if you take a look at Chris Noth, and you've got to wonder, he's never around when there's a Klan rally.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>Could he be in the Klan.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: No, why would he be in the Klan.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: No? Well, I think he might.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Why.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: You know what, the point is, he accuses us, --</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Yes.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: -- conservatives, of being racists with only by using his own psychological intent. He has no evidence, whatsoever. So, I don't need evidence to say that he's in the Klan.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: So, you're going to sink to his level.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: Oh, I'm going to exceed sinking to his level.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>I'm diving head first into the deep end and calling him a Klan man.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: So, by extension, are you a &quot;Pinhead of the Week,&quot; too.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: Yes. By the way, literally, a pinhead right here.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>And this is -- notice the similarities?</p>
<p>O'REILLY: No, I don't.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: Well, look closely.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: No, I don't.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: No. But point is this, if they can do it, --</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Yes.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: -- so can we.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK, very immature. All right, now, McGuirk --</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: Yes, Bill.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: -- has selected the Dallas Texans fans. Go.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FOOTBALL GAME ANNOUNCER: Shot goes down. Chris Long. And the ball is out.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE SPORTS REPORTER: He goes down awkwardly on his right ankle. He comes out of the game. Texans fans actually cheering as he comes out.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FOOTBALL GAME ANNOUNCER: Ball dead for a sack but no fumble. And Schaub is reaching for that right ankle.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right. They were cheering. That's in Houston, Texas, Dallas, Texas AFL Team.</p>
<p>So, the fans were cheering because Matt Schaub, who's a very good quarterback and has done very well in Houston, --</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: In the past.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Yes.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: Not this year.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: He's having a rough year this year. But he's hurt.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: Yes. No, it's just the disgrace and, you know. I mean, with these guys, the ones -- it wasn't all the fans. It was some of them. The ones who did --</p>
<p>O'REILLY: It was loud enough that you could hear the cheer.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: Yes, that's right. And they are what totally what rhymes with &quot;astros,&quot; which is asinine yoyos. But it's not just Houston that has a problem.</p>
<p>In San Francisco, over the weekend, the 49er fans, they wave while that opposition player was lying injured on the field.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Yes.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: It's just another manifestation of the dark side of sports. I hope --</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I don't know if it's the dark side of sports so much as the bread and circuses that has come into America from Ancient Rome.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: Hmmm.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I really believe that happening. We're becoming so de- sensitized. And if you don't win right now, you're garbage.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: Right, right.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: I mean, yesterday, I read a letter -- and this is serious, from a guy who said John McCain was a Communist. I mean, you know, you go, &quot;How is this happening.&quot;</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: I heard a guy say John McCain was like the new Hanoi Jane, which is totally offensive.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I think we're really getting out of control here. All right, my pinheads are across-the-board in the media. Roll the tape.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE REPORTER: Congress trying to come to an 11th hour- deal just to kick the can further down the road.</p>
<p>BRIAN WILLIAMS, NBC ANCHOR: And what about the fact, Senator, that the solution now kicks the can down the road.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE NEWS ANCHOR 1: I'm against this deal. I think it's a bad deal. This is a kick-the-can-down-the-road deal.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE NEWS ANCHOR 2: There's no structural reforms. There's no cost savings. It's kick the can down the road.</p>
<p>JOHN KING, CNN NEWS ANCHOR: There's no indication, given the policy divide. And, now, they've kicked the can into an election year.</p>
<p>ANDERSON COOPER, CNN HOST: Will this -- I mean, we're just kicking the can down the road. Is this all going to happen again.</p>
<p>STEVE ISRAEL, REPRESENTATIVE, NEW YORK (D): Well, you know, Anderson, we are kicking the can. But better to kick the can than to stomp on the can.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>O'REILLY: Now, I'm going to kick some butt.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>You, morons, have got to stop this cliche-ridden garbage. You guys are on national television. Stop with the cliches, will you.</p>
<p>I don't want to hear the &quot;goal posts moving.&quot; I don't want to hear the &quot;light is at the end of the tunnel.&quot; I don't want to see, &quot;We have to have that conversation.&quot;</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>I don't want to hear it. I don't want -- and you're in charge on your dopey show, &quot;The Fox Five.&quot;</p>
<p>GUTFELD: Yes, I am.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: If they do that on &quot;The Five,&quot; you get up immediately )and stop it.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: I banned this two years ago.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I heard it on your show this week.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: Why can't we kick the can up the road.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: No, we can't -- we're not kicking anything, no cans, we're not kicking any butts. We're not kicking, all right.</p>
<p>Now, the reason I do this, and they're all pinheads -- the reason I do this -- since you're too lazy to think about what they want to say, all right, if you object to the policy not being reformed immediately, then say that.</p>
<p>You're driving me crazy, all right. I may have to listen to talk radio if this continues.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>GUTFELD: Don't do that.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Well, McGuirk works in talk radio, you know.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: That was a shock.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Does Imus kick the can. Is he kicking can in the morning.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: But I think the can means something else.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: By the way, he's running a half-marathon this week. And I don't see you're aware of that.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Imus is running a half-marathon?</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: Indeed, he is.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: In his living room. He doesn't leave the house, does he.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: Well, I'll let you know how it turns out on Monday. But he's, you know, he's not kicking anything. He's kicking butts.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: He's kicking cans.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: But we all agree that this has got to stop, correct.</p>
<p>MCGUIRK: Yes.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: It has --</p>
<p>GUTFELD: No, it will never stop. It will never stop.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Even if I put you in charge.</p>
<p>GUTFELD: Yes. Hey, I'm incompetent.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right. Well, Gutfeld finally telling the truth.</p>
