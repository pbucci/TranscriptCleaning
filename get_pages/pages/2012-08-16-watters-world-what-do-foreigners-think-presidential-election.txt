			<h1 itemprop="headline">Watters' World: What do foreigners think of the presidential election?</h1>
			<div class="article-info">
			<div> 		
			 		<time itemprop="datePublished" pubdate datetime="2012-08-15T00:00-04:00">Published August 15, 2012</time><div itemprop="sourceOrganization" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization">FOX News</div></div>

			 <div class="m video-ct" data-video-id="1787528703001">
							<a href="http://video.foxnews.com/1787528703001/what-do-foreigners-think-presidential-election" itemprop="url"><img	src="http://a57.foxnews.com/global.fncstatic.com/static/managed/img/fn2/video/876/493/081512_of_watters_640.jpg?ve=1&tl=1" itemprop="image"></a>

<p><em>This is a RUSH transcript from &quot;The O'Reilly Factor,&quot; August 15, 2012. This copy may not be in its final form and may be updated.</em></p>
<p><em>Watch &quot;The O'Reilly Factor&quot; weeknights at 8 p.m. and 11 p.m. ET!</em></p>
<p>BILL O'REILLY, FOX NEWS HOST: &quot;Watters' World&quot; segment tonight. About 8 million people currently live here in New York City. About 3 million of them were born in other countries. So we asked Jesse Watters to hit the streets to find out what some of those folks think about the presidential campaign.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>JESSE WATTERS, O'REILLY FACTOR PRODUCER: Where are you from.</p>
<div></div>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Dominican Republic.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Panama City, Panama Canal.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: Beijing.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Israel.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Mexico City.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: From India.</p>
<p>WATTERS: India.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED IDE MALE: Yes.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Congratulations.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: For.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Being from India.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I don't know what that means. I don't know what that means.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: I was born in Iran.</p>
<p>WATTERS: So, you, guys, are trying to get the nukes.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: Yes.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>WATTERS: Try to slow that down, okay.</p>
<p>BEN STILLER, HOLLYWOOD ACTOR: Bomb, bomb, bomb, bomb, bomb, bomb, bomb.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: Puerto Rican and Dominican. And she's Dominican.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: Macedonian. The country in Europe.</p>
<p>WATTERS: I knew that. I'm very very good with Geography.</p>
<p>JEFF BRIDGES, HOLLYWOOD ACTOR: Yes, well, you know, that's just like your opinion, man.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Naturalized citizen.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Yes.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: I'm under working visa, so I don't think I can vote, can I.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Maybe these days.</p>
<p>CHRIS FARLEY, HOLLYWOOD ACTOR: Voting kicks ass, right.</p>
<p>(APPLAUSE)</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: (Speaking in another language)</p>
<p>WATTERS: I don't speak Spanish.</p>
<p>WILL FERRELL, HOLLYWOOD ACTOR: In English, please.</p>
<p>WATTERS: What do you do for a living.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I'm a professional body guard. You mess with me, I break your back.</p>
<p>WATTERS: You are making me nervous.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Why do you hate me.</p>
<p>WATTERS: President Obama, how do you feel about him.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: I don't like him.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>WATTERS: Obama.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Who.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: The fellow playing first base for St. Louis.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: He doing not so good, you know. He's trying to do something but he's not really good right now, you know.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I'm supporting Obama because, I think, he is working for poor people.</p>
<p>WATTERS: What's so good about Obama.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: He's different.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>WATTERS: He's different.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: Yes.</p>
<p>WATTERS: That's for sure. What do you like about the President.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: About who.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I'm telling you who is on first</p>
<p>WATTERS: Now, you're from a Communist nation. Do you think Obama is a Communist.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: I don't think so.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Who.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Obama.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: That's the man's name.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: I guess, he is trying to turn this into a Socialist country.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Obama is not a Communist. How do you know that.</p>
<p>JOE PESCI, HOLLYWOOD ACTOR: Are you sure.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Now, is he Christian or is he Muslim.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I think he's a foreigner.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Who.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Obama.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Who is on first.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I don't know.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: His name is Hussein. That's a Muslim name. Christian names are bible names.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Anyone can call him whatever they choose to call him. I call him not good enough for me.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I said it and I ain't taking it back neither.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Mitt Romney, what have you heard about him.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: The only thing I know is he used to live one time in Mexico.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I like his attitude. Just like me.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Yes, what's that.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Serious and don't play.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: He's your textbook Republican</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: We're going to vote for Mitt Romney.</p>
<p>WATTERS: You are.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Definitely.</p>
<p>WATTERS: There's nothing wrong with having a lot of dough, right.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: If the guy got so much money, why would he be president. That's a lot of stress.</p>
<p>MIKE MYERS, HOLLYWOOD ACTOR: Feel extreme relaxation.</p>
<p>WATTERS: When I say the word &quot;Republican,&quot; what do you think of.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: Pepsi.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Is that a great new Pepsi can or what.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Are you a fan of THE FACTOR.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Very big fan of THE FACTOR. Bill O'Reilly is the man.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: Hi, Bill. I love you.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: There's nothing that Obama do is good but then Bill O'Reilly, all right.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I mean, you've got to wise up.</p>
<p>WATTERS: What's the best part of the show. &quot;Watters World.&quot;</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: &quot;Watters World.&quot;</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Nice. Not thrilling but nice.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: Hello. My name is Giselle. Hi.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Awww Bow wow wow wow wuh.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>WATTERS: Every time we end these, you shake your head.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I know, Watters. The audience is going to blame me, not you, all right. When you went out there, the consensus was Romney or Obama.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Seventy-thirty Obama. The Obama is an emotional connection with the ethnic background. He reaches out to poor people in the amnesty play. But, I tell you what, Romney's father was born in Mexico.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Right.</p>
<p>WATTERS: I had a lot of Latin American immigrants tell me that. They know about that.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: They know that.</p>
<p>WATTERS: So that's making an impression.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: When you go out and you talk to people who aren't born in the United States -- a lot of people know more than the regular folks who were born here.</p>
<p>WATTERS: I know.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK. They know more about what's going on. They've kind of a heightened interest in the news of the day. Did you find them.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Yes. The taxicab drivers listen to a lot of radios, so they're always tuned in. Some of the interviews I did -- you know, it's spring break, I'd say, &quot;What do you think about Mitt Romney.&quot; And they'd say, &quot;Who.&quot; No one said that this time. So, these guys are pretty --</p>
<p>O'REILLY: So, the one guy who didn't know who Barack Obama was -- the Abbot Costello guy who kept going back, that was a put on.</p>
<p>WATTERS: No, I think that was a language barrier. I think it's a language barrier. The guy was from Colombia.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK, so what language doesn't understand Barack Obama.</p>
<p>WATTERS: I don't know.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: What language doesn't understand that.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Maybe it was the way I was pronouncing it. Who.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right. Watters, everybody, unleashed. With apologies to Joe Biden.</p>
