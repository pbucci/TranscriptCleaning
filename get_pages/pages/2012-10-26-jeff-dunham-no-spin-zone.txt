			<h2><a href="http://www.foxnews.com/on-air/oreilly/index.html">Interviews</a></h2>
			<h1 itemprop="headline">Jeff Dunham in 'No Spin Zone'</h1>
			<div class="article-info">
			<div> 		
			 		<time itemprop="datePublished" pubdate datetime="2012-10-25T00:00-04:00">Published October 25, 2012</time><div itemprop="sourceOrganization" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization">FOX News</div></div>
			</div>		
			
			<div class="social-count">
					<div>
		                <a class="fbk" href="#"><span>Facebook</span></a><span class="fbk">0</span>
		                <a class="twt" href="#"><span>Twitter</span></a><span class="twt">0</span>

	            </div>           
	            
            <div itemprop="articleBody">			
			 <div class="m video-ct" data-video-id="1925141383001">
							<a href="http://video.foxnews.com/1925141383001/jeff-dunham-in-no-spin-zone" itemprop="url"><img	src="http://a57.foxnews.com/global.fncstatic.com/static/managed/img/fn2/video/876/493/102512_of_dunham_640.jpg?ve=1&tl=1" itemprop="image"></a>
							<div class="caption">
								<p itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization" itemscope="" itemprop="sourceOrganization"><span itemprop="name" class="source"></span></p>

						<p></p>
<p><em>This is a RUSH transcript from &quot;The O'Reilly Factor,&quot; October 25, 2012. This copy may not be in its final form and may be updated.</em></p>
<p><em>Watch &quot;The O'Reilly Factor&quot; weeknights at 8 p.m. and 11 p.m. ET!</em></p>
<p>O'REILLY: &quot;Back of the Book&quot; segment tonight. One of the hottest comedians in the country, our pal ventriloquist, Jeff Dunham. He has a variety of dummies who are very eager, very eager to comment on the presidential election.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEOTAPE) </strong></p>
<p>JEFF DUNHAM, VENTRILOQUIST: You, guys, got into another argument on the phone today, didn't you.</p>
<div></div>
<p>WALTER: She calls right back and she goes, &quot;Did you hang up on me?&quot;</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>I said, &quot;I don't know. Did it sound something like this. Click.&quot;</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: So, Walter, I wanted to talk to you because you're one of the folks and you are supporting Governor Romney for president. Why.</p>
<p>WALTER: Well, I think there's all kinds of reasons. There's all kinds of social reasons. He's a smart guy. He's a good-looking guy, don't you think.</p>
<p>I mean, I think looks has a lot to do with it. I think he's got a lot of things that we shall talk about. I mean, with $16 trillion in debt. Do you know how much that is, Bill.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Sixteen trillion.</p>
<p>WALTER: Sixteen trillion. That's more zeros than they have hosts on MSNBC. Do you know what I'm saying.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I think I do.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>WALTER: Mitt Romney is a guy that, I think, we can get behind. He's a strong man, he's a good leader. The same on the vice president side of things.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: When, Walter, you watched the debate the other day --</p>
<p>WALTER: Yes.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: -- and you saw the President not being in command, what did you think.</p>
<p>WALTER: I thought to myself, he should be sitting on someone's knee.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: That's harsh though.</p>
<p>WALTER: Yes, I think it's the truth. Do you see any similarities between me and Joe Biden.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Forehead. WALTER: Because there has been an email going around that Joe Biden and I look exactly the same. It's kind of a frightening thing. Do you know what the difference is between me and Joe Biden.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: What.</p>
<p>WALTER: Well, the guy that I work for knows what's going to come out of my mouth. Do you know what I'm saying.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I see. Nobody knows what's going to come out of the Vice President's mouth.</p>
<p>WALTER: Exactly.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Now, when you go to vote, Walter --</p>
<p>WALTER: Yes, sir.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: -- do you object to showing a photo ID.</p>
<p>WALTER: No, I don't.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK. So, you would happily show a photo ID in order to get into the polling place.</p>
<p>WALTER: What the hell is wrong with showing a photo ID, Bill. What is the problem with this. I don't understand why you shouldn't show a photo ID. You shouldn't be in this country without a freaking photo ID.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: It expresses the vote.</p>
<p>WALTER: It does not suppress the vote. It makes the ugly folks not show up. If you don't have that, you don't speak the language, you don't have the ID, go home.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK, so your theory is it does suppress the vote because it discourages ugly people showing an ID to vote.</p>
<p>WALTER: Yes, now, you're twisting my words. All I'm saying is you should have an ID. Right, you got me on that one, Bill. Well, you're a smarter guy than you look.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: That's for sure.</p>
<p>DUNHAM: It's Dunham.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Not when you look at it. It says Dun Ham, Jeff Dun Ham. Ham. You're the other white meat.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Here's Peanut. And Peanut is a supporter of President Obama. Correct, Peanut.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Absolutely. I'm a supporter of President Obama. Of course I am. He's a great president. He's one of the greatest presidents we've ever had.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: What is the thing about President Obama that you like the most.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Well, first of all, image is important. I know Walter said looks is important but that's not it. Image, Bill. Image is important for the whole world.</p>
<p>He's the leader of the free country. Look at this guy. He is cool, don't you think so. The guys has drones and a kill list, you know what I mean. This is a cool guy.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: What about the economy, Peanut. It's not really doing that well.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Well, I don't know about the economy, Bill. I mean, unemployment, those numbers came out and it's below 8 percent for the first time in years.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: And you know what, it's about --</p>
<p>PEANUT: And that's even after Jim Lehrer quit after the first two minutes in the debate.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: That's right. But unemployment among puppets is about 35 percent.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Oh, wait. Ha, ha, ha, ha.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Oh, I see. You're mocking me.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Yes, I understand he's trying to get rid of PBS. That's what Romney is trying to do. He doesn't want PBS anymore.</p>
<p>I will speak for Walter. He is for getting rid of PBS. Do you know why.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Why.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Walter says that he wants a $1.35 for a Big Bird. He's going to go to Chick-fil-A.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK, he's going to get it cooked.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Yes.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK, do you object to showing an ID when you vote.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Well, Bill, you know, I love it when you ask dumb questions.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>Do you think that I need to show an ID. Look at me.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: You are pretty unique.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Yes, I am pretty unique. And that's another reason I like Obama. He doesn't have a birth certificate either.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: So, you both have something in common.</p>
<p>PEANUT: Yes, no one knows. Are we from Hawaii. Are we from Kenya. I don't know. Are we from Mars. It doesn't matter. All I know is that I have a tag that says, &quot;Inspected by number 4.&quot;</p>
<p>O'REILLY: But when you vote in a few weeks --</p>
<p>PEANUT: Yes.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: -- they are going to ask you for an ID.</p>
<p>PEANUT: And I love the fact that you think I vote, Bill.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>Thanks for believing in the magic.</p>
