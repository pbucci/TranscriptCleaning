			 		<time itemprop="datePublished" pubdate datetime="2013-08-21T00:00-04:00">Published August 21, 2013</time><div itemprop="sourceOrganization" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization">FOX News</div></div>

						<p><em>This is a RUSH transcript from &quot;The O'Reilly Factor,&quot; August 21, 2013. This copy may not be in its final form and may be updated.</em></p>
<p><em>Watch &quot;The O'Reilly Factor&quot; weeknights at 8 p.m. and 11 p.m. ET!</em></p>
<p>O'REILLY: Thanks for staying with us. I'm Bill O'Reilly. In the &quot;Miller Time&quot; segment tonight, if you are a convict behind bars in California, you can now get cable TV and other perks in one Northern California prison if you pay for them.</p>
<p>More evidence the country is doomed. The sage of Southern California joins us now from Santa Barbara.</p>
<p>You believe this, Miller, Fremont, California, it's in the Bay Area -- so, you're in for misdemeanor and they say, &quot;Hey, you've got a hundred and fifty, we'll give you pizza,&quot; you know --</p>
<div></div>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>DENNIS MILLER, FOX NEWS CONTRIBUTOR: Billy, Billy, one second.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Whoa, yo.</p>
<p>MILLER: One second, Billy, Billy, I've got a foreign tourist on fire in the mini-studio.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>Sparky, come here.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>Sparky, get away from Olaf. Get -- come here.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>Olaf, shut up. I'm on the O'REILLY FACTOR. Come here, Sparky.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: You're not going to save him. You're not going to save Olaf.</p>
<p>MILLER: Sorry, Billy, I've got something just hit the fan. Olaf, shut up. Go ahead.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right, prison, where you may be going, --</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>-- cable TV, perks for a hundred, a hundred fifty a day.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>You know, come on. What's going on, Miller.</p>
<p>MILLER: Well, listen, I'd probably take advantage of this hundred and fifty-five bucks a night. Well, put it this way, if I slept on my back, I wouldn't pick up the fee.</p>
<p>If I slept on my stomach, I'd pay the hundred and fifty-five. That's just me. I'd use some of my frequent-felon miles.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>All I know is this, if I can slip a guard a double sawbuck and be --</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>-- sexually-compromised by a better looking inmate, I'm there with it.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right. I don't understand any of that. But I like the frequent-felon miles.</p>
<p>MILLER: Well, put it this way, if my cellmate looked like Carville, I'm springing for the extra $155.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: That's the truth, I guess.</p>
<p>MILLER: Carville makes Slade Gorton look like Tyrone Power.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Another oblique reference to an astronaut, I believe. Look, --</p>
<p>MILLER: No, Slade Gorton was a -- was a House rep or a senator or something.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: So, you don't even know who he was.</p>
<p>MILLER: Don't you remember that Skeletor guy.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Wasn't there an astronaut named that. I think it was an astronaut named that.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>MILLER: All I know is that Carville looks like a muppet accidentally washed on hot.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Oh, geez. All right, let's get off that topic to the Hillary Clinton zone. Any way she could convince you --</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>-- to vote for her for president.</p>
<p>MILLER: Well, listen, if it's going to be her versus Christie, they both better start running before they decide to run --</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>-- because they're both getting a little exhausting and I don't think you can take a year and a half and trek around the country and not have a heart attack in the shape they're in.</p>
<p>If they shoot that debate between Christie and Hillary, they better put it in IMAX. But I can tell you this, Hillary is in fine form. When you see us, the Clinton Foundation spent $50 million on travel.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>So, she's in game shape and the money over there has been laundered more frequently than Joan Crawford's peignoirs. If they can canonize Barack Obama, they can certainly deify Hillary.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>And there might be the added benefit that she might be a little crankier with terrorists than this guy because Hillary is pissed off at the old man. Because she knows that we know she has been cheated on more frequently than a blind woman playing scrabble with gypsies.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>So, maybe, she should be cranky with Hezbollah.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: That doesn't answer the question. Could you possibly vote for her.</p>
<p>MILLER: No.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK, that does answer the question.</p>
<p>MILLER: No, come on. I could've said it right off the bat but we've got a fill eight minutes.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right. Now, that's true.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>And I'm glad you led me into the final.</p>
<p>MILLER: No, I'm not voting for Hillary.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: There's no way? Nothing she can do.</p>
<p>MILLER: No, come on.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Personal appearance at your house, nothing. Would you save Hillary or your dog. No, don't answer that. Don't answer that.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>MILLER: I don't even have a dog and I'd save the dog.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: But you could rent one just for the day.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>MILLER: I'd get a Kelly Dog from a temp agency.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Right.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>Just so you could make the decision. Bradley Manning, eight years, because that's what it looks like what he's going to get.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>They gave him 35 out in -- you know, if he behaves in Leavenworth. What do you say.</p>
<p>MILLER: Well, I feel bad for Archie Manning because Eli and Peyton are such good kids and it's obvious --</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>-- that he went off the track there.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Not the same family. Not the same, no, no.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>MILLER: Oh, I'm sorry. I misappropriated that. Listen, you better not wear that beret into prison because if you spy in prison, you know, our off-disparage correctional facilities, that's at least one place where they still make Solomonesque sense.</p>
<p>I trust warlock and cellblock D over John Roberts at this point as far as jurisprudence goes. And he starts ratting people out in prison. This will all take care of itself, old Bradley Manning.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Yes, I don't think he's going to do that. And, you know, Leavenworth, that's a hard labor facility for the -- it's not the hundred and fifty a night and we'll give you the big screen TV and the little hors d'oeuvres.</p>
<p>MILLER: No, very hard labor. You can beg -- you can beg for an epidural in Leavenworth and they won't give you one.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: That's right. So, this isn't easy time but he should have --</p>
<p>MILLER: Oh, the other labor. Sorry, all right.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right, yes, Miller.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>Thank you. That's all I have to say to you tonight.</p>
<p>MILLER: Billy, I've been away on -- hey, wait a second. Olaf, shut up.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>I've got this foreign tourist. He was drinking over a Ricola juice and it caught his horn on fire. Come here, Sparky. Get away from the foreign soil. Come here.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
