			<h1 itemprop="headline">Donald Trump Upset About GOP Debate Criticism</h1>
			<div class="article-info">
			<div> 		
			 		<time itemprop="datePublished" pubdate datetime="2011-12-08T00:00-05:00">Published December 08, 2011</time><div itemprop="sourceOrganization" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization">FOX News</div></div>

			 <div class="m video-ct" data-video-id="1317199008001">
							<a href="http://video.foxnews.com/1317199008001/donald-trump-upset-about-gop-debate-criticism" itemprop="url"><img	src="http://a57.foxnews.com/global.fncstatic.com/static/managed/img/fn2/video/876/493/120811_factor_ingraham_640.jpg?ve=1&tl=1" itemprop="image"></a>

<p></p>
<p><em>This is a RUSH transcript from &quot;The O'Reilly Factor,&quot;December</em></p>
<p><em>08, 2011. This copy may not be in its final form and may be updated.</em></p>
<p><em>Watch &quot;The O'Reilly Factor&quot; weeknights at 8 p.m.</em></p>
<p><em>and 11 p.m. ET!</em></p>
<p><strong>BILL O'REILLY, HOST:</strong> &quot;Impact&quot; segment tonight, as you may know Donald Trump has been</p>
<div></div>
<p>selected by News Max to moderate a Republican debate in Iowa at the end of</p>
<p>December. That is causing some controversy. Right now Mitt Romney, Ron Paul,</p>
<p>Rick Perry and Jon Huntsman say they will not participate. Rick Santorum and</p>
<p>Newt Gingrich say they will. Michele Bachmann undecided. And Mr. Trump doesn't</p>
<p>like the criticism he's getting from some Republicans.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN AUDIO CLIP)</strong></p>
<p><strong>TRUMP:</strong> Frankly I know a lot more than, you know, a lot of</p>
<p>the so- called pundits that you read about. Karl Rove is an example. What did</p>
<p>he do? He gave you Bush and he and Bush crashed and burned and to be honest,</p>
<p>Laura, nobody could have won that election in all fairness to John McCain</p>
<p>because of what took place during the Bush Administration and that was Karl</p>
<p>Rove.</p>
<p><strong>(END AUDIO CLIP)</strong></p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> All right. With us now here in Washington is Laura</p>
<p>Ingraham who had Mr. Trump on her radio program earlier this week. So he's</p>
<p>pretty teed off about this whole thing.</p>
<p><strong>LAURA INGRAHAM, FOX NEWS CONTRIBUTOR:</strong> Yes, I... I just gave</p>
<p>him a call a few minutes ago. I talked to him.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> You can get Trump on the line, right.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> I can. I used your name. I said it was Bill</p>
<p>O'Reilly calling and they took the call immediately. So I chatted with him for</p>
<p>a few minutes. And I said, look why are you so angry? You seem angry on my</p>
<p>show.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> Yes.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> And he said you know why I'm angry? Because the</p>
<p>country is going to hell.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> Right.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> And we don't seem to want have a serious debate</p>
<p>about some of the big issues facing our country which I think he wanted to have</p>
<p>at this debate. China, what do we do the about the rise of China and not just</p>
<p>discussing it in broad terms. What specific trade policies are going to be put</p>
<p>in place so we even the playing field as China cheats, big issue.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> Full disclosure I know Trump pretty well and</p>
<p>we go to some Yankee games together.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Yes.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> But I don't understand why they won't show up. And</p>
<p>if I were running for president on the Republican side I would want Trump</p>
<p>asking me questions.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Well and he's a Republican...</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> I think that... because here is the advantage to</p>
<p>the Republicans.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Yes.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> And I don't know why they are not going on. Even</p>
<p>if they don't watch the debate, it's going to be on every Web site, your show,</p>
<p>my show, everything.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Yes.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> And there is Trump. But he's going to give you a</p>
<p>little jazz. You can give it right back to him. It's not like its Wolf Blitzer</p>
<p>and you can't make fun of Wolf. You can make fun of Trump.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> And but... but Trump also, I think he would ask</p>
<p>questions. Like ok, how will we know that your presidency won't be like a third</p>
<p>Bush term? I think he thinks it's important to have a debate about what</p>
<p>happened preceding this Obama Administration.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> Yes.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> How did it turn out that we go from eight years of</p>
<p>Bush to Barack Obama. That's why he is angry I think.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> Yes but the Republican contender should be able to</p>
<p>handle Donald Trump...</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Yes... if you can't handle Donald Trump then good</p>
<p>luck of going against the billion dollars of Barack Obama. I think -- I think...</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> Well, yes.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> I think everybody was like, oh it's a reality</p>
<p>show.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> I don't know why they are not. I sympathize with</p>
<p>Trump on this one.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> He's -- he's a good brand manager Bill.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> Right.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> And if the Republican Party was as good at brand</p>
<p>managing they would do a better job.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> News Max a conservative outfit, they asked Trump</p>
<p>to moderate, I think it's more interesting than to have... you know the usual</p>
<p>people and, you know, not to disparage any journalist.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Right.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> But Trump brings in a lot more heat.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Business, negotiation. He knows how to negotiate.</p>
<p>And Mitt Romney, remember, is really good on China from Trump's perspective. He</p>
<p>said look we might have to put tariffs on China because the marketplace</p>
<p>globally is totally unfair now and skewed because China is cheating. So it's</p>
<p>not a fair marketplace. Romney would have been good in that debate.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> All right. Two weeks ago Herman Cain was a player.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Yes.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> And now he is not. Almost every person who</p>
<p>supported Mr. Cain has gone to Newt Gingrich. And it isn't so much ideology</p>
<p>it's that Gingrich is on the ascent.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Yes.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> Whereas Michele Bachmann and... and Rick Perry</p>
<p>they are staying the same single digits, so the Perry... the Cain people said,</p>
<p>you know, it's not too far from Herman Cain to Newt Gingrich so we'll go over</p>
<p>to him. And that's why his poll numbers have skyrocketing.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Yes well I was digging into this with my callers</p>
<p>today, Bill. And I was trying... what is it? What is it? Why is Gingrich on the</p>
<p>rise? And it really is coming into play what we talked about a few weeks ago.</p>
<p>The people want to believe that there are going to be big things happening to</p>
<p>transform this government. That it's not going to be a third Bush term. That</p>
<p>it's going to be something fundamentally different.</p>
<p>And a lot of the people who were around and... and from 2000</p>
<p>to 2008 when God bless them George Bush was in power and especially in those</p>
<p>past few years was doing a lot of really liberal things, those people are most</p>
<p>of the ones who are really angry about the rise of Newt Gingrich.</p>
<p>Newt Gingrich can say look, I wasn't there in the 2000s. Yes</p>
<p>I was out there making a lot of the money. But I balanced this budget. I got</p>
<p>Bill Clinton to move to the right on welfare reform. We did some big things.</p>
<p>I'm not perfect but we did some really transformational things.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> Well, certainly he's got a story to tell but I</p>
<p>believe these polls are going to level out. I think that Romney has got a hard</p>
<p>core support of about 23 percent, 24 percent there are a couple of more</p>
<p>debates. One Saturday, right? Isn't there one Saturday?</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Yes, yes.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> And then the big Fox News a week from tonight.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Right.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY</strong>: And that's going to be huge. And Romney has really</p>
<p>got to step up.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> He's got to fight for this.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> He's got to, absolutely.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> It can't be a coronation for Mitt Romney. I think</p>
<p>people think, ok, you think you're entitled to this. And he said the other day</p>
<p>to his credit I have to fight for this, I have to fight for every vote.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> He's got to... he's got to really differentiate</p>
<p>himself from Gingrich in a way that attracts people.</p>
<p><strong>INGRAHAM:</strong> Evangelicals are critical in Iowa.</p>
<p><strong>O'REILLY:</strong> All right. Laura Ingraham everybody.</p>
