			<h2><a href="http://www.foxnews.com/on-air/oreilly/index.html">Interviews</a></h2>
			<h1 itemprop="headline">Miller Time: Two shutdowns</h1>
			<div class="article-info">
			<div> 		
			 		<time itemprop="datePublished" pubdate datetime="2013-10-02T00:00-04:00">Published October 02, 2013</time><div itemprop="sourceOrganization" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization">FOX News</div></div>

		                <a class="fbk" href="#"><span>Facebook</span></a><span class="fbk">0</span>
		                <a class="twt" href="#"><span>Twitter</span></a><span class="twt">0</span>

			 <div class="m video-ct" data-video-id="2715205712001">
							<a href="http://video.foxnews.com/2715205712001/miller-time-two-shutdowns" itemprop="url"><img	src="http://a57.foxnews.com/global.fncstatic.com/static/managed/img/fn2/video/876/493/100213_reilly_miller_640.jpg?ve=1&tl=1" itemprop="image"></a>

						<p><em>This is a RUSH transcript from &quot;The O'Reilly Factor,&quot; October 2, 2013. This copy may not be in its final form and may be updated.</em></p>
<p><em>Watch &quot;The O'Reilly Factor&quot; weeknights at 8 p.m. and 11 p.m. ET!</em></p>
<p>O'REILLY: Thanks for staying with us. I'm Bill O'Reilly. In the &quot;Miller Time&quot; segment tonight, we have three hot topics for you, beginning with the shutdown of the two Hillary Clinton movies.</p>
<p>You may remember, CNN and NBC said they both might produce films about Mrs. Clinton. But I was skeptical.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<div></div>
<p>O'REILLY: I'm going to predict that neither movie is going to be made before 2016. I don't think it's going to happen.</p>
<p>Look, CNN is trying to come back from Armageddon, ratings Armageddon. They're trying to build up their shop over there. They don't need this. They don't need this.</p>
<p>NBC is already labeled the far-left network. They already have problems in there.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>All right, last week, CNN and NBC said they were dropping the films. Miller is otherwise occupied this evening but I talked with him last night.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEOTAPE) </strong></p>
<p>O'REILLY: So, Miller, am I an oracle or what.</p>
<p>DENNIS MILLER, FOX NEWS CONTRIBUTOR: Well, you know, Billy, I think observers in the scene saw Hillary in her post-Secretary of State revelry --</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>-- as we should say. And they begin to sense that this thing might balloon into an iMax project.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>And they just didn't have that in the budget, so I think you're an astute observer, as oppose to an oracle.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Yes, well, you're talking about maybe that they -- no, I'm not even bothering with that.</p>
<p>MILLER: You know why they shut it down?</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Why, Miller.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>MILLER: They didn't want the mini-series bias for Hillary to get in the way of their regular nightly newscast bias for Hillary.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>So, they had to make this tough call.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: They're just saving it up. OK, I got it. Now, government shutdown doesn't really affect you out in Santa Barbara.</p>
<p>I mean you're living large, you're on the beach, you're chasing the pelicans, whatever the hell you do out there, --</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>-- I don't know. But the government shutdown doesn't really affect you, does it.</p>
<p>MILLER: No, Billy. I don't know that it affects the 800,000 people laid off from the government in Washington D.C. Folks, I don't know if you've heard, you can get two years unemployment now.</p>
<p>And they shut the zoo down, so all you leftists who are pro-animal, listen, that zoo was cruel. You should view this and see the silver lining in here. I'm not worried about the shutdown.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>I'm worried about when they start it back up.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>And I don't think the Republicans are handling this well either. Did you watch that wild jammy party two nights ago, Billy?</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Sure.</p>
<p>MILLER: Not exactly Sandy telling Russo she's dating Danny Zuko. The thing about the Republican brain thrust is, I don't trust their brains.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>And they better realize, the guy who holds the bully pulpit righ now is an actual bully. And, lastly, I want to mention that anytime something is named after a guy, i.e., ObamaCare, and he doesn't want to sign up for it, that's a really bad tea leaf, OK.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: That is a big -- a bad sign. Now, --</p>
<p>MILLER: I mean, if I was him, I'd come on, just hold a presser and say, &quot;Listen, I want to talk to you about MeCare, not ObamaCare, MeCare.&quot;</p>
<p>And I'd take a laptop and I'd sign in and become participant number one. He'd lay waste to the Republican Party for the next 40 years.</p>
<p>Just say, &quot;I'll sign up for it.&quot; But he won't. And that tells me everything I need to know.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Now, the polls say that the Americans are holding Republicans, more than Democrats, accountable for the shutdown. Are you surprised by that.</p>
<p>MILLER: No, that doesn't surprise me, Billy. I think our time has passed, man. We're like a Norman Rockwell Litho.</p>
<p>I think that we live in a country now where it's pretty well- understood that the one physical malady that won't be covered under ObamaCare is busting your ass, all right.</p>
<p>And we might not crash this now but it's going to crash itself down the road. And I kind of like what Ted Cruz --</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>-- did, just sending up a signal flare and said, &quot;We aren't part of this.&quot; I think it's basic stuff, it's checkbook stuff, Billy.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>You've got to have the stubs equal the checks that are going out. We don't do that now.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: No, we don't do it.</p>
<p>MILLER: If that's the way the country wants it, so I lock the pod face on.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Now, were you surprised, your pal from Saturday Night Live, Rob Schneider, now has defected --</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>-- the Democratic Party and gone over to the Republicans.</p>
<p>MILLER: All I know is this, eventually, if you're using common sense, --</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>-- and you're not locked in to zealotry and you just look at the way the country is going right now and say, &quot;We've got to spend what we take in. We can only spend that,&quot; you're not a zealot coming. You're just a -- you're a pragmatist.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Right.</p>
<p>MILLER: Maybe you just got pragmatic, for God's sakes.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Well, as you get older, I mean, you've got a balance of budget, you've got a plan and, yes, you see things for the craziness it is.</p>
<p>MILLER: Yes.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: And in your state, I mean how insane is it out there. It's every regulation in the world. You put peanut butter on, they'd come in and say, &quot;How much.&quot;</p>
<p>MILLER: I know.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: And what kind of bread you have to use. And, you know, -- I mean, it's nuts.</p>
<p>MILLER: Billy, I feel like, in the state of California, and I am getting older by the way, my prostate has turned into a jujube. But I feel that, in the State of California, I might turn all my funds over to Sacrament because I have -- I think I'll have more access to them as a petitioner as I do the actual proprietor.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: That's right. You will get more money back.</p>
<p>MILLER: So, maybe, I'll go that way since we're getting more than half now. Yes.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right, finally, did you see the &quot;60 Minutes&quot; on &quot;Killing Jesus&quot; --</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>-- and me, your humble correspondent. Did you check that out.</p>
<p>MILLER: I've watched it on a perpetual loop since it happened. Hey, here's the difference --</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>-- between me and you, Billy. I'm a bit of a lapse Catholic. Like last time I went to confession, I said, &quot;You first.&quot; But I know when the Holy Spirit visits you in the middle of the night, he tells you to write books that will sell 10 million books.</p>
<p>When the Holy Spirit visits me, he says, &quot;Buy O'Reilly's next book retail.&quot; Then he just flies out of the room.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>That's the difference between our Gods.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Don't you think that, maybe, the Holy Spirit, both you and I, when we're on stage at the &quot;Bolder-Fresher&quot; show, I mean don't you think there might be a little (inaudible) coming down there.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>MILLER: I think the Holy Spirit says, &quot;I wish I was God the Son, so I could make a buck and get into that VIP line to meet these two geniuses.&quot;</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Yes, sure he does.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>A little blasphemy from Miller. We apologize. Dennis Miller, everybody. There he is.</p>
