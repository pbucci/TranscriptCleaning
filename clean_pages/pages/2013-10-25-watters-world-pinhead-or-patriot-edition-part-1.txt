			<div class="article-info">

						<p><em>This is a RUSH transcript from &quot;The O'Reilly Factor,&quot; October 24, 2013. This copy may not be in its final form and may be updated.</em></p>
<p><em>Watch &quot;The O'Reilly Factor&quot; weeknights at 8 p.m. and 11 p.m. ET!</em></p>
<p>O'REILLY: &quot;Watters' World Segment&quot; tonight the revival of &quot;Pinheads and Patriots.&quot; As you may know the seeds of American liberty were planted in Boston, Massachusetts. So we sent Watters to Faneuil Hall to ask the folks about modern day &quot;Pinheads and Patriots.&quot;</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>WATTERS: Pinhead or patriot?</p>
<div></div>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Pinhead.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Well he is the President.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Pinhead. All that change is really not changing.</p>
<p>WATTERS: You don't like the President?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: No the guy is weak.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: He is one of the best presidents we have ever had since Jimmy Carter and Bill Clinton.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Oh my God.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Patriot.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Patriot. Why a patriot?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: He is the President. You can't disrespect the President.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I'm smart and I want respect.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: I think he's a nice chap but he is being handed a poisoned chalice.</p>
<p>WATTERS: You're a poet or are you just English?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Yes, baby.</p>
<p>WATTERS: How is Obamacare working out so far?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Good, great --</p>
<p>WATTERS: Pinhead or patriot?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: That's Mitt Romney, right?</p>
<p>WATTERS: Do you want to be in his binder full of women?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: I don't think so. I think I would get squished.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Pinhead or patriot?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: This is Tom Carrie.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Tom Carrie?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE: Give me a break.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Who is Tom Carrie running against?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE: Wasn't that like Hillary Clinton?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE 1: Patriot.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Patriot?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE 1: Yes.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Why do you say that.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE 1: I believe he knows business.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Pinhead or patriot.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 2: Patriot.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Patriot. Yes, you voted for him?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 2: Yes.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 3: His heart's in the right place.</p>
<p>WATTERS: OK.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 3: I just don't think his mouth is.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 4: Oh, man.</p>
<p>WATTERS: You know who this is.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 4: Umm, is it Gore.</p>
<p>(BUZZER)</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 5: Yes, that is Mitt Romney. Why did he run for the presidency. I do not know.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>WILL FERRELL, ACTOR: I love scotch. I love scotch.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>WATTERS: So, pinhead or patriot.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 6: Gross.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>WATTERS: You look like you can filibuster for about 10 straight hours.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>JIM CARREY, ACTOR: That's never happened to me before. I must be tired.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE 1: You know what, I think he's a patriot.</p>
<p>WATTERS: You do.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE 1: Yes. He's standing up for what he believes in.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 7: Not related to Tom Cruise, huh.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Not related to Tom Cruise.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>TOM CRUISE, ACTOR: Great.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 2: I do like what the Tea Party stands for so I'm going to go patriot.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 3: Is that Ted Cruz.</p>
<p>WATTERS: That is Ted Cruz.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 3: Well, he is looking chubbier.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>TED CRUZ, SENATOR, TEXAS (R): I'm a big fan of eating White Castle burgers.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>WATTERS: Do you know who this is.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 8: Yes, I do.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Who's that.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 8: Who do you think it is.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Who do you think it is.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIIFED MALE 8: I'm not sure.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>LOU ABBOTT, ACTOR: I don't know. Third base.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>WATTERS Watch your step.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>JAMES STEWART, ACTOR: What I need is a couple of good, stiff drinks. How about you.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>WATTERS: Pinhead or patriot.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 7: Patriot. I respect her, too.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Who is it.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 7: I have no idea.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 2: Well, I'm from California, so --</p>
<p>WATTERS: I'm sorry to hear that.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 2: -- pinhead.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MUPPET: You'll get no argument from me on that.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 6: Patriot.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Patriot.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 6: Yes.</p>
<p>WATTERS: So, you know who this is.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 6: Who is he.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE 2: I don't know who that is.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>WATTERS: You've never seen her before?</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE 2: No. That's a hard one.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 2: She's the House Minority Leader.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Word.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED FEMALE 1: And if politicians should have to read a bill before they vote on it, then it's time for her to go.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED ACTOR: Go, go on!</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 3: Patriot. She's just so strong. And if she heard me say anything else, she'd probably come hunt me.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>MARLON BRANDO, ACTOR: You can act like a man. What's the matter?</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>WATTERS: Most people could kick your butt though.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 3: I'm pretty sure of that. I mean, who do you call.</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>KATHERINE HEIGL, ACTRESS: Wait, what do you mean.</p>
<p>(END VIDEO CLIP)</p>
<p>(LAUGHTER)</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 5: Pinhead. One, she's a Republican.</p>
<p>WATTERS: She's actually a Democrat.</p>
<p>UNIDENTIFIED MALE 5: Is she.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEOTAPE) </strong></p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right now, Watters is here. We're going to continue that on Monday.</p>
<p>WATTERS: That's right, part two.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: And I understand, I'm involved --</p>
<p>WATTERS: You're involved.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: -- in the pinhead and patriot --</p>
<p>WATTERS: It's even funnier than this one. And Biden is involved, too, so it's great, you know.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right. Me, Biden, who else.</p>
<p>WATTERS: You, Biden -- I think Harry Reid is involved.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Reid.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Boehner.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Uh-huh.</p>
<p>WATTERS: You know, all the --</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All the heavyweights.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Yes, the big guys. The big guns.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Now, when we make fun of other people, we're doing it an affectionate way.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Of course.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I mean, you know -- because that's just a lot of characters.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Right.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: I mean, the guy with the Yankee shirt is a character.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Right. I think he's still drunk from the night before.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: He looked like he did.</p>
<p><strong>(BEGIN VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>You know, he's relaxed.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Right, right.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: How many people refused, flat out refused to talk to you when you go into a form like this.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Well, the --</p>
<p>O'REILLY: That percentage.</p>
<p>WATTERS: -- percentage, I'd say maybe 10 percent.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Do they all know THE FACTOR. Do they all know --</p>
<p>WATTERS: No. Sometimes, it's just the way I look, or if I'm holding cards or something like that.</p>
<p><strong>(END VIDEO CLIP) </strong></p>
<p>O'REILLY: OK. And if you have the collar up in the back.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Yes, that would just repel people automatically.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: And I guess that's about 30, 30 percent, you know, look.</p>
<p>WATTERS: That's right.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: But, generally speaking, what percentage of the people know THE FACTOR. When you say, THE O'REILLY FACTOR, what does it -- what percentage.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Know THE FACTOR, I'd say about 50 to 60 percent.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Of those people?</p>
<p>WATTERS: Yes, yes. And, you know, we tabulated some of these answers. Pelosi, two to one called her a pinhead. And only a third knew who she was.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: A third.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Ted Cruz, only one person out of 14 people knew who Ted Cruz was.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Really, only one of 14.</p>
<p>WATTERS: And that -- yes. And he was determined to be a pinhead, two to one, OK.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: Uh-hmm.</p>
<p>WATTERS: And then we're going to have your tabulation on Monday. So, check that out.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: It will be interesting to see how many people knew me.</p>
<p>WATTERS: Yes.</p>
<p>O'REILLY: All right. Jesse Watters, everybody. Once again, Monday night, we will do part two of &quot;Pinheads and Patriots.&quot;</p>
