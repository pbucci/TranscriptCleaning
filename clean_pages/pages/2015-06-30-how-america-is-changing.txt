<p><b>O'REILLY: </b>I only have a minute left so .</p>

<p><b>KRAUTHAMMER:</b> . but it's a sociological fact.</p> 
<p><b>O'REILLY:</b> OK. 30 seconds on number two, 30 seconds on number three, too.</p>

<p><b>O'REILLY: </b>And the parents have abdicated their child rearing responsibilities.</p>
